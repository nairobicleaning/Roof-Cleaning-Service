## Roof Cleaning Methods

Different roof types require specific cleaning techniques to avoid damage and achieve optimal results.

### 1. Soft Washing

Soft washing uses low-pressure water combined with cleaning solutions to remove dirt, algae, and mold without damaging roofing materials. It is ideal for delicate surfaces.

### 2. Pressure Washing

Pressure washing is used for durable roofing materials such as concrete tiles and metal sheets. It effectively removes stubborn dirt and debris.

### 3. Manual Cleaning

Manual cleaning involves scraping and brushing to remove moss and debris, followed by rinsing and treatment.

These techniques are selected based on roof condition and material to ensure effective **Roof Cleaning Service Nairobi**.
